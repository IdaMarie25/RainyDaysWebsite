For Kids

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? Come on down to our museum, there´s plenty to see and do.

Are you ready for an amazing journey of discovery and fun?

Build your own Lego village

Step into the world of imagination and engineering in our Lego Village! Whether you're building a cozy house, a busy train station, or a travel back in time to meet the mammoths, our Lego station lets you create and design anything your heart desires. Test your skills, and see how your creations come to life with real-life engineering principles!

You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore.

Dive into the world of programming

Have you ever wanted to create your own video game or control a robot? In our Programming Zone, you can learn how to code in fun, interactive ways. From writing your first line of code to making your own animations, our programming workshops are a great way to unleash your inner tech genius. The future of technology starts here!

During the school holidays we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. To find out more about the holiday club and how you can join, send us a message.

Discover science in our hands-on lab

Get your lab coat on and get ready for some awesome experiments! Our Science Lab is packed with activities that let you explore chemistry, physics, biology, and more. Whether you're mixing colorful liquids, exploring the mysteries of space, or testing out cool machines, there's always something new and exciting to try.

We also have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.
