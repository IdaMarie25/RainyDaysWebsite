Ecology

Hey there! Did you know that everything on Earth—from the air we breathe to the water we drink—works together in an amazing way? This is called ecology!

Just like how your LEGO creations need different pieces to come together, nature needs all its parts to stay healthy. Let’s dive in and explore why ecology matters!

Building a Sustainable Future for the Planet

As we learn more and more of the impact humans are having on the planet, the more important it becomes that scientists explore how to create a sustainable future not just for humans but for the whole planet.

In our ecology section of the museum, we unpack the dangers we currently face including global warming, extinction of species, and pollution. But we also focus on the positive ways we can all contribute to a brighter future.

Look at our snowglobe exibition, and how massive snowstorms effect the environment in real time

Did you know that about 70% of the Earth's surface is covered by water, but only 3% of that water is fresh and drinkable? The rest is salty ocean water!

Global warming disrupts ecosystems, threatening biodiversity and causing extreme weather patterns, like heatwaves, droughts, and stronger hurricanes, which impact the whole planet.

Extreme weather events disrupt ecosystems by destroying habitats, wiping out species, and altering food chains.
